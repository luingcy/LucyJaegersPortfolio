Game Design

GravityBound

Gravitybound is an isometric puzzle game developed in Unity with a high focus on visual complexity. I initially created GravityBound independently as a prototype, and was later assigned a team of people to produce it into a full game. As director of the project I managed it from start to finish, overseeing all creative decisions and development work.

Alongside this I also:
● was the sole 3D artist for the project, creating over 120 unique models for the game, most of which were voxel models
● wrote the story
● worked as the primary scripter, coding everything aside from the main menu functions designed 16 of the 25 levels.

Icarus

Icarus is a perspective shifting puzzle game developed in Unity. I created it independently as a prototype, and was later assigned a team of people to produce it into a full game. As director of the project I managed it from start to finish, overseeing all creative decisions and development.

Alongside this I also:
● worked as the sole scripter for the entire project
● designed 2 out of the 5 levels
● modeled a portion of the games assets, including the character model.

Animation

Personal Bumper

A 20 animation I created using After Effects and Maya. The animation is a reflection of my own artistic style based on three pillars I selected; Ethereal, Subdued and Ambient.

Still Life

A 3D render based on personal objects from my childhood. The project was to imitate ‘Still Life’ photography. In particular I was heavily inspired by ‘Vanitas’ paintings and wanted to create something closer to an old painting than a photograph.
